This is your master administration control panel for the QuizPro Platform.

From here, you have root access to modify everything in the database. Use the top navigation bar to access different modules.

Tips for navigation:

  • 💡 Organized Questions: Instead of searching through mixed questions, click on Quizzes above, and click the Edit (pencil) button on any specific quiz. The questions for that quiz will be shown and editable directly at the bottom of the form!
  • 🔍 Powerful Filters: In each section, you can use the built-in search bar and filters to quickly find specific data (e.g. filter Questions by a specific Quiz title, or search Users by Email).
  • 👥 User Management: You can promote Students to Creators or demote users in the Users module.
